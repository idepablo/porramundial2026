Sistema de Puntuación
Points are cumulative — an exact score earns all three bonuses. Bonus multipliers (×2 or ×3) can be applied by the admin to any match.
★ BONUS
Admin can mark any match as ×2 or ×3
The admin selects 1 (normal), 2 (double points) or 3 (triple points) for any match across all rounds. For example a match worth 6 pts becomes 12 pts (×2) or 18 pts (×3). Applies to the combined score of sign + goal difference + exact result.

Extra Time & Penalties Rule
• If no draw at 90' → the 90' result counts.
• If draw at 90' → the 120' (extra time) result counts, even if it stays the same score.
• Example: 0-0 at 90', 1-0 at 120' → 1-0 counts.
• Example: 2-2 at 90', 2-2 at 120', then penalties → 2-2 counts. Predicting 2-2 earns sign + goal diff + exact score.
• Team advancement point is scored on who actually advances, regardless of penalty shootout.
